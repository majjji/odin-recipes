Chipa

Description

This is a traditional paraguayan dish called "chipa", it is neither a dessert or main meal. Chipa is a food that is eaten at any moment of the day,it can be your breakfast, dessert, afternoon snack or as we say in spanish, "merienda". A simple dish but with amazing reach, it is in my opinion the perfect recipe.

You will need only a few ingredients and a lot of work on the mass with your hands you will have a perfect and delicious kind of bread dish. I recommend to eat chipa with coffee or with cocido which is a delicious paraguayan drink that goes perfect with it.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of cassava flour
  • 1/2kgof cornflour
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 8 eggs
  • 800gr of cheese
  • 1/2 cups of milk
  • Anise and salt as you wish

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs with the butter until they're perfectly integrated
  2. Add the anise and salt, a tablespoon will be perfect but you can add as much as you want
  3. Add the cassava flour to the mix and keep blending the mass. While blending add a little bit of milk, as necessary,to make the mass consistency not too wet but not too dry, it should be in the middle ground.
  4. Add the corn flour and keep adding the milk as you blend
  5. As you keep mixing, add the cheese and don't stop blending until the mass is as previously stated, in the middle ground
  6. Once the mass is well integrated and with a perfect consistency, not too dry nor wet, grab small portions of it and make the shape you wish.
  7. Preheat the oven at 180C (Celsius degree), bake at 280C for around 25 minutes
  8. Now enjoy your delicious chipa and don't forget to share with others!
